Plot

SPOILER ALERT!

In a not so distant future, the world is slowly starving and at the brink of collapse due to a seemingly incurable disease killing all food crops. Cooper, a former test pilot turned farmer, is recruited to pilot humanity's last ditch effort to find a new home. Through a newly discovered worm-hole, leading to another solar system with several potential planets for colonization, Cooper and his crew must decide which planet will be humanity's new home. With only one ship, if they choose wrong, it will be the end of the human species. Adding to his emotional turmoil, due to the nature of space travel, Cooper has to struggle with leaving his family and possibly never returning within their lifetime.
